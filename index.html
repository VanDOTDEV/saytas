MY CEBU-BOHOL TOUR JOURNEY

View Full Journal (PDF)

UP CEBU INCUBATOR FOR IT

Observation: UP Cebu Incubator helps tech startups grow by providing office space, mentorship, and connections to investors and industry experts. It’s an essential resource for emerging tech companies in Cebu.

Learnings: Collaboration with the government, universities, and investors can create opportunities that foster innovation and growth. Incubators play a significant role in nurturing startups and empowering entrepreneurs.

DYNATA PHILIPPINES INC.

Observation: Dynata specializes in data collection and analytics. They provide businesses with insights to understand consumer behavior and market trends more effectively.

Learnings: Using data to guide decisions is crucial for business growth. This experience taught me the power of analytics and the importance of gathering valuable data to improve products and services.

RIVAN IT CEBU

Observation: Rivan IT Cebu provides training and development for tech professionals, focusing on critical areas like cybersecurity, networking, and cloud technologies.

Learnings: I learned how crucial it is to continuously develop skills in the ever-evolving IT industry. Having a strong foundation in key areas can lead to a long-lasting career in tech.

MATA TECHNOLOGIES, INC.

Observation: Mata Technologies is a leader in virtual reality for tourism and real estate. They create immersive experiences for potential buyers and tourists to explore properties and destinations remotely.

Learnings: This experience taught me the potential of VR in changing industries. It’s a great way to bring people closer to the experience of a product or service without being physically present.

T.a.R.S.I.E.R 117

Observation: T.a.R.S.I.E.R 117 is a local emergency response team that works to help people during accidents or natural disasters in Bohol. They coordinate with various sectors to ensure safety and timely interventions.

Learnings: This experience showed me the importance of community collaboration and preparedness in disaster management. It reinforced the need for training and the role of tech in emergency responses.
